Xu Zhao

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Dalian University of Technology in 2010 and my Ph.D. in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in 2019. Since 2019, I have been working at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I am currently an Associate Professor.
